
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly evolving field of computer science focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding. AI technologies are being integrated into various industries, revolutionizing the way we live and work by enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making processes. As AI continues to advance, it holds the potential to unlock new possibilities and address complex challenges across different domains.
